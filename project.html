Taking Action(s)

is a participatory project, inviting you, the individual, to undertake a small assignment; a brief instruction generated randomly from our custom built task-generator. Contribution is open to anyone and can come in many forms; text, image or object based; documentation, donation, spoken or drawn; emailed, delivered, transferred or sent.

Collective contributions will then be aggregated, sorted, ordered, printed, multiplied, stored, modified, collated, collected and manifested in an attempt to find collective patterns, synergies or a meaningful whole.

The project will continue to evolve throughout its duration at West Space from 5 March to 6 April, 2014, where Lizzy Sampson and Herve Senot will work towards a collective outcome by taking the actions of many and collating them as a single, evolving outcome.
