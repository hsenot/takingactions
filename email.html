Hello and thanks for contributing to Taking Action(s).

Taking Action(s) will be exhibited at West Space gallery from 7 March - 5 April, 2014 and is a project by Lizzy Sampson and Herve Senot.

You are free to interpret this task in any way you like, however contributions must then be submitted via email to contribute@takingactions.org.

Your contribution can take the form of:
  • Images
  • Text
  • Links
  • Video
  • Sound
  • Scanned documentation
  • Process-based documentation
  • Documentation of an outcome
  • Description
  • ...
or anything else that can be sent via email.

What happens to your contribution?

Your contribution will be logged in our Action Bank, then form part of the material to be aggregated during the course of the Taking Action(s) exhibition. Each day, Lizzy Sampson and Herve Senot will work to explore the possibility of a cohesive meaning in the accumulation of contributions within the Action Bank. Your contribution will be on display within the gallery intermittently. An evolving workspace will allow for contributions to be displayed primarily in 2D form on the wall.

Anonymity or credit?

We don't know who you are unless you tell us when submitting your contribution. You and your contribution can remain anonymous, however we are most happy to credit each contributor. If you wish to be credited, please include your name when emailing through your contribution.

Every contribution will inform our project and form part of The Findings, a publication which will be distributed to all contributors.

Thanks again - we very much look forward to your contribution.
